Meal Replacement/Protein Shake

Description

Quick and easy to make meal replacement shake that suits all health and fitness goals

Ingredients

  • Plain Greek Yogurt
  • Almond Milk
  • Oats
  • Banana
  • Flaxseed (optional)
  • Other fruits to your liking (optional)

Steps

  1. Blend 1/2 cup of oats and 1/2 cup of almond milk until smooth
  2. Add greek yogurt - add based on protein requirements
  3. Add Banana and optional ingredients
  4. Blend all ingredients until smooth
  5. Serve on ice
