The Best Top Ramen Recipe

Description

Everyone knowns about Top Ramen. We're going to make it better

Ingredients

  • Top Ramen - Beef or Chicken Recommend
  • Powder seasoning (Garlic, onion,cayenne pepper, etc)
  • Pepper sauce - Trini Scorpion sauce recommended
  • Hot dog
  • Eggs
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Chives

Steps

  1. Add Top Ramen flavouring along with Powder Seasonings, Pepper Sauce, Soy Sauce and Sesame oil to taste
  2. Boil Ramen in 3 cups of water
  3. Add Slices of Hot Dog Sausage after two (2) minutes of boiling
  4. Add beaten eggs with approximately one (1) minute of boil time left
  5. Add Ramen with eggs and sausage to bowl of flavouring and oil; stir until ingredients are evenly distributed
  6. Garnish with thinly sliced chives
